MapItOut Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

MapItOut respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

MapItOut does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

All process maps, nodes, links, colours, notes, layouts and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is uploaded to the developer.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide the app's functionality, including saving and organising your process maps.

Third-Party Services

  • No analytics
  • No advertising
  • No user accounts
  • No cloud storage
  • No third-party tracking

Children's Privacy

MapItOut does not knowingly collect personal information from children or users of any age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
