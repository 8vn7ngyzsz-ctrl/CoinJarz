Impact Zero Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Impact Zero respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

Impact Zero does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information.

Game data such as scores, personal bests, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in storage systems.

How Your Data Is Used

All locally stored data is used only to provide gameplay features, save progress, and maintain personal best records. This information is never transmitted to the developer.

Third-Party Services

Impact Zero does not use:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics services
  • User accounts
  • Cloud storage services
  • Third-party tracking technologies

Children's Privacy

Impact Zero does not knowingly collect personal information from children or users of any age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
