ChainTimer Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

ChainTimer respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

ChainTimer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

All timer data, notes, settings, preferences, and alarm selections created within the app are stored locally on your device using Apple’s built-in storage systems.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to provide the functionality of the app and is never transmitted to the developer or any third parties.

Notifications

ChainTimer uses Apple's local notification system to alert you when timers finish. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are not sent through any external server operated by the developer.

Third-Party Services

ChainTimer does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • User accounts
  • Cloud storage services
  • Third-party tracking technologies

Children’s Privacy

ChainTimer does not knowingly collect personal information from children or users of any age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
