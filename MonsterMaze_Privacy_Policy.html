MonsterMaze Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

MonsterMaze respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

MonsterMaze does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Any game data created while playing, such as game settings, preferences, or progress, is stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in storage systems.

How Your Data Is Used

Local data is used only to provide the game's functionality, such as remembering your settings or saved progress. This information never leaves your device and is never shared with the developer or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

MonsterMaze does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • User accounts
  • Cloud storage services
  • Third-party tracking technologies

Children's Privacy

MonsterMaze does not knowingly collect personal information from children or users of any age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
